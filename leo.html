What the media says about Leo and his global award at Future Minds Hackathon 2025

👤 About Me

I’m Leonardo Tronci, a 4th-year student at the European Classical High School of the Convitto Nazionale in Cagliari, living in Quartucciu (Sardinia, Italy).
I am passionate about technology, especially computer engineering applied to health and education. I created Leo EmoPet to help children better manage emotions using digital empathy and gamified virtual therapy.

I also volunteer with Arka Eventi Culturali, supporting social and educational campaigns – including Christmas fundraising for Syrian children.

🗞 What They Say About Leo

Italian media described my journey as a mix of empathy and creativity that brought me from my hometown to a global AI stage. Winning 3rd place at the Future Minds Hackathon 2025 confirmed my desire to innovate through technology, helping real people with real needs.
